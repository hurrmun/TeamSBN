What is Grocey?

With rising standards of living all accross the world, food wastage in households has reached an all time high due to excess buying and food expiration. SMART Grocery List aims to help consumers purchase just enough groceries to reduce wastage and still prepare enought hearty meals for the family.

More Information

Many households over-buy grocery items, causing them to expire or go stale. Many items are also not used and are stored indefinitely or thrown away. Hence, we decided to create Grocey, a smart grocery list that gives an estimate of how much food you should be buying.

Grocey takes information that you provide such as number of family members, how many times you cook in a week, age, lifestyle, etc. to generate a grocery list with just the right amount of ingredients such that there is no wastage.
